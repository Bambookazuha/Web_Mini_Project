Article

An article is a written piece intended to inform or entertain readers about a specific topic. It is usually factual, structured with a clear introduction, body, and conclusion, and often includes headings or subheadings for easy navigation.

Formal Letters

A formal letter is a professional type of communication that follows specific conventions in tone, structure, and language. It is often used for business or official matters, such as job applications, complaints, requests, or inquiries.

Poem

A poem is a form of creative writing that uses rhythm, structure, and often rhyme to evoke emotions, create vivid images, or tell a story. It relies on figurative language, such as metaphors and similes, and often explores deep or abstract themes like love, nature, or personal reflection..
