B1 Reading

Improve your reading with us!

Tips for Effective Reading

  • 1. Read regularly to improve your reading skills.
  • 2. Pay attention to new vocabulary and try to use it in sentences.
  • 3. Ask questions about the text to check your understanding.
  • 4. Try to visualize the story or information in your mind while reading.
  • 5. Discuss what you've read with friends or classmates to deepen your understanding.

Lesson 1: At the Beach

Text: Last Saturday, I went to the beach with my family. We arrived early in the morning to avoid the crowds. The sun was shining, and there was a light breeze. We spent the day swimming, building sandcastles, and playing beach volleyball. I enjoyed having a picnic by the water. In the afternoon, we took a walk along the shore and collected seashells. It was a perfect day.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. Why did the family arrive early at the beach?
  • 2. What activities did the family do at the beach?
  • 3. What did they do in the afternoon?

Lesson 2: The Importance of Reading

Text: Reading is an important habit that can help you in many areas of life. It can improve your vocabulary, make you more knowledgeable, and help you become a better writer. Books open your mind to new ideas, and they can also be a great way to relax. Whether you enjoy fiction or non-fiction, reading is a valuable activity that everyone should make time for.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. What are some benefits of reading mentioned in the passage?
  • 2. How does reading improve your vocabulary?
  • 3. Why is reading considered a relaxing activity?

Lesson 3:The City Park

Text: The city park is a great place to visit when you need a break from the busy streets. It has many trees, a large lake, and walking paths. People go there to exercise, relax, or meet friends. In the summer, there are often outdoor concerts and events. The park is a peaceful escape from the noise of the city.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. What can people do in the city park?
  • 2. What happens in the park during the summer?
  • 3. Why is the park a good place to escape the city?

Lesson 4: A Healthy Diet

Text: Eating a balanced diet is essential for staying healthy. You should eat a variety of foods, including fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein. Avoid too many sugary snacks and drinks. Drinking plenty of water is also important. A healthy diet can help you feel better, have more energy, and reduce the risk of health problems in the future.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. What are the key components of a healthy diet?
  • 2. What should you avoid eating too much of?
  • 3. How can a healthy diet help you in the long term?

Lesson 5: My Hometown

Text: I grew up in a small town by the sea. It’s a quiet place with friendly people. The town has a small beach, a few shops, and a park where children play. In the evenings, people gather at the café to chat. Though the town is not very big, it has a special charm, and I always feel at home when I visit.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. Where did the writer grow up?
  • 2. How do people spend their evenings in the town?
  • 3. What makes the town special for the writer?

Lesson 6: Traveling Abroad

Text: Anna went to the museum with her family last weekend. They looked at paintings from famous artists and sculptures from ancient times. Anna was particularly interested in the dinosaur exhibit, where she learned about the different species that once roamed the Earth. It was a day full of learning and fun.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. What challenges might travelers face?
  • 2. How can different cultures impact the travel experience?
  • 3. Why is traveling abroad a good way to expand your horizons?

Lesson 7: A Memorable Birthday

Text: Last year, I celebrated my birthday at a theme park with my friends. We went on all the roller coasters and ate lots of cotton candy. In the afternoon, we watched a live show and played games. My friends gave me a special gift, and we ended the day with a big cake. It was the best birthday I’ve ever had.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. Where did the writer celebrate their birthday?
  • 2. How did the writer feel about the birthday celebration?
  • 3. What was the highlight of the day?

Lesson 8: The Importance of Exercise

Text: Exercise is important for maintaining a healthy lifestyle. It helps to improve your heart health, increase energy levels, and keep your body strong. Regular exercise can also help reduce stress and improve your mood. Whether it’s walking, running, or doing yoga, finding an activity you enjoy can make exercising fun and rewarding.

Comprehension Questions:

  • 1. What are some benefits of exercise mentioned in the passage?
  • 2. What is one way exercise can improve your mental well-being?
