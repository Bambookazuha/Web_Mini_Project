A2 Writing

Improve your writing with us!

  • Use simple and clear sentence structures (e.g., Subject + Verb + Object).
  • Focus on everyday vocabulary, like describing places, hobbies, and routines.
  • Use linking words like "and," "but," "because," and "so" to connect ideas.
  • Write in short paragraphs with 3-4 sentences each.
  • Practice describing things in the present and past tense.

Time words: always, sometimes, every day, yesterday
Places: park, school, shop, home
Actions: play, eat, watch, visit, write

1. Writing About A Day

Write 5-6 sentences about your daily routine. Use time words like *always* or *every day.*

2. Writing About A Place

Describe your favorite place in 3-4 sentences. Use words like *big, small, beautiful, quiet.*

3. Writing A Short Story

Write a short story about something you did yesterday using past tense.

4. Writing About Hobbies

Write 4-5 sentences about your hobbies. What do you like to do? Why?

5. Writing About School

Write 5-6 sentences about your school.What do you like about your school?

6. Writing About Food

Describe your favourite food in 5-6 sentences. Why do you like it?
