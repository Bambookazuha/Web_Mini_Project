C2 Writing

Master your writing techniques with us!

  • Adopt a formal tone and structure when necessary (e.g., academic writing).
  • Incorporate nuanced vocabulary like *consequently, notwithstanding, elucidate*.
  • Use balanced arguments, addressing multiple perspectives with cohesion.
  • Focus on clarity and precision while avoiding redundancy.

Exercise 1: Advanced Persuasion

Write a letter to a local council advocating for stricter environmental policies. Use persuasive language and advanced connectors.

Exercise 2: Synthesis Writing

Combine ideas from two articles about the pros and cons of remote work. Summarize the key points and present your conclusion.

Exercise 3: Persuasive Writing

Write a persuasive essay convincing your reader why students should or should not have homework. Use strong arguments, examples and evidence.

Exercise 4: Feature Article

Write a feature article on a topic of your choice, aiming to inform and engage your readers. You could write about a current social issue, a notable historical event, an influential person, or a breakthrough in science or technology.
