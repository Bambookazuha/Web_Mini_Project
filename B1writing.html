B1 Writing

Improve your writing with us!

Tips

  • Use a variety of sentence structures, such as compound and complex sentences.
  • Incorporate transitional words like *however*, *therefore*, and *in addition*.
  • Organize your ideas into clear paragraphs with a main idea and supporting sentences.
  • Check for grammar and punctuation accuracy after writing.

Useful Vocabulary

Exercise 1: Writing a Formal Email

Write an email to your teacher asking for advice on how to improve your writing skills. Use formal language and appropriate sentence structures.

Exercise 2: Write About a Problem

Write 150 words about a problem in your community and suggest possible solutions.

Exercise 3: Writing a Short Story

Write 150 words story about the best day of your life.

Exercise 4: Write an Invitation Letter

Write a birthday party invitation letter consists of 100-150 words to your friend.
